Customer Behaviour

People are starting to care more about animals and the environment. More customers are choosing cruelty-free brands because they want to make kinder, safer choices. Companies are starting to listen and change their ways too.

How Can You Help

You can help by only buying from cruelty-free brands. Look for labels like the leaping bunny. You can also tell your friends and family, and support companies that care about animals.

Why It Matters

Animal testing can cause pain and suffering to innocent animals. Choosing cruelty-free means we care about kindness and safety. Today, there are better, non-animal ways to test products. When we choose cruelty-free brands, we show companies that we want beauty without harm. It's a small choice that makes a big difference.
